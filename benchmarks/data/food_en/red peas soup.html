Red peas soup is a soup eaten in Jamaica. It is made of kidney beans (known locally as red peas), seasonings such as scotch bonnet pepper, pimento seeds, etc. Red Peas Soup is eaten usually eaten with yam and Jamaican dumplings.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jamaican-recipes.com/redpeassoup.html |title=Red Peas Soup |publisher=Jamaican-recipes.com |date= |accessdate=2013-09-01}}</ref><ref>[1]{{dead link|date=September 2013}}</ref>
