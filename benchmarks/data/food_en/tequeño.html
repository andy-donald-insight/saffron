Dedito de Queso also known as Palitos De Queso or Teque単o is a fried breaded cheese stick or a spear of bread dough with queso blanco (white cheese) in the middle. It is formed into a breadstick and then usually fried in oil for 2 to 3 minutes at {{convert|400|F|C}}. It is one of the most popular in many cultures snack foods in parties, especially weddings.

There are many theories and legends about the origin of the name Teque単o.
