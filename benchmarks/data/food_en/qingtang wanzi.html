{{no footnotes|date=August 2009}} Qingtang wanzi (清汤丸子) is a traditional meatball soup found in Beijing cuisine.

Preparation

Traditionally, this dish includes the main ingredients: 300 grams of tenderloin, 50 grams of egg white, wood ear{{Disambiguation needed|date=July 2011}}, oil, cooking wine,<!--is it Shaoxing?--> sesame oil, cilantro, green onion,<!--is it scallion?--> and ginger.

The tenderloin, ginger and green onions are minced and made into balls by mixing with egg whites. The meatballs are boiled until they float to the surface and then taken out and served with coriander and sesame oil. Vegetables are served together with meatballs.<!--Which kind of Chinese meatball?-->
