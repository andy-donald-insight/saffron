Tohax ({{lang-kk|тоқаш}}, {{lang-ky|токоч}}, {{lang-ug|توغاچ}}, {{Lang-uz|Samarqand noni/Самарқанд нони}}), also known as toqach or toghach, is a type of tandoor bread. It is consumed within the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region<ref>Xinjiang Restaurant</ref> of China, as well as in many regions of Central Asia (Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan).<ref>В.&nbsp;В.&nbsp;Похлебкин, Национальные кухни наших народов, Москва, Центрполиграф, 2004, ISBN 5-9524-2783-9, ISBN 978-5-9524-2783-9 (William Pokhlyobkin, National Cuisines of our Peoples, Moscow, Centrpoligraf, 2004).</ref>
