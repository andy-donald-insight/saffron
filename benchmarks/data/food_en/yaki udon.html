{{nihongo|Yakiudon|焼きうどん||"fried udon"}} are thick, smooth, white Japanese noodles eaten with a special sauce, meat and vegetables. It is simple to make and a popular dish in Japan. It originated in Kokura, Fukuoka Prefecture after the Pacific War.
