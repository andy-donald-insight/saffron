Kakara pitha(odia:କାକରା ପିଠା) is a sweet deep-fried cake from Odisha offered to temple deities and served hot or cold on many festivals.<ref>http://www.odiakitchen.com/atta-kakara-pitha/</ref&gt;

Ingredients

kakara pitha
  • Atta (Wheat Flour) – 300&nbsp;g
  • Chinni (Sugar) -250&nbsp;g
  • Nadia (Coconut) – ½ a Coconut(shredded)
  • Gujurati( Cardamom) – 4 pcs
  • Chhena-100&nbsp;g
  • Edible camphor-a pinch
  • Black pepper- little and crushed
  • Luna (Salt) – As per taste
  • Refine Tela( Refined Oil)- for deep frying
