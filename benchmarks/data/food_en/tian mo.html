Tian mo (甜沫) is a traditional breakfast soup from the city of Jinan in the Shandong province of China. The soup is made of millet power, peanuts, vermicelli, cowpea, spiced tofu (or shredded tofu skin), and spinach.<ref>Tian mo Baidu wiki, Retrieved 21 September 2012</ref> The soup has a thick texture once cooked and has a salty taste. It is normally eaten with Youtiao.
