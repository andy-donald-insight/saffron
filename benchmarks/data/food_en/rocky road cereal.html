{{no footnotes|date=March 2013}} Rocky Road was a breakfast cereal, created and sold by General Mills. The cereal was made up of balls of cornmeal, chocolate-covered cornmeal, and chocolate-covered marshmallows. Advertising for the product consisted of three cartoon characters who embodied the three constituents of the cereal noted above.
