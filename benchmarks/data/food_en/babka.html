{{Wiktionary}} Babka may refer to:

  • Babka (bread), a Polish sweet, leavened bread made with a rich dough (often containing raisins), which is made for Easter Sunday to celebrate the rising of Christ

  • Babka (cake), a sweet yeast cake
  • Babka (genus), a genus of fish, of which the sole member is the racer goby
  • Babka River, a river in Russia
  • Babka (surname)
