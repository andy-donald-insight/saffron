Processed cheese spread containing chilli paste

Cheese spread is a soft, spreadable, cheese or processed cheese product which can be packaged in many ways: in slices which can be individually wrapped in plastic or not, in small foil-wrapped triangles or squares grouped together in a cardboard container, in a pressurized can in which the cheese product comes out in a string, in a jar in semi-liquid form, as a solid in a butter-like bar, in plastic tubs, and in many other ways.
