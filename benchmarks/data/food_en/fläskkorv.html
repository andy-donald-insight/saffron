Fläskkorv ({{lang-sv|Pork sausage}}) is a Swedish sausage made largely or entirely from pork.<ref>Travel Stockholm, Sweden: An Illustrated Guide, Phrasebook, and Maps. Published. MobileReference, 2007.</ref> Traditionally it is sold raw (though now it is not uncommon to find precooked varieties), and often served during the Christmas smörgåsbord (Julbord).

A typical accompaniment is rotmos, a dish made from mashed carrots, potatoes and rutabagas (swedes).
