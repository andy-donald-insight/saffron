{{Infobox prepared food | name = Es teler | image = | alternative names = | caption = A bowl of es teler | country = Indonesia | region = Nationwide | creator = | course = Dessert | served = Cold | main_ingredient = Shaved ice, coconut, avocado, jackfruit, condensed milk | variations = | calories = | other = }} Es teler is a fruit cocktail in Indonesia. Avocado, coconut meat, cincau, jackfruit and other fruits are served with coconut milk, sweetened condensed milk, Pandanus amaryllifolius leaf (normally in the form of cocopandan syrup), sugar, and a tiny amount of salt.

This concoction, created by Murniati Widjaja, won a competition in 1982 to come up with a national drink for Indonesia.<ref>Es teler</ref>
