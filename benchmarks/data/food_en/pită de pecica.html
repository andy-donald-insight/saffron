Pită de Pecica<ref>{{ro}}Pita de Pecica, de pe vremea dacilor, produs traditional cu patalama de la UE, 26 October 2009, eco magazin, 3 March 2012</ref> is a Romanian round bread, roasted directly on hearth, specific from the town of Pecica in Arad County.

From 1980 to 1989, Pită de Pecica arrived daily on the table of dictator Nicolae Ceaușescu, who was impressed by its taste. It was transported by jet plane daily from Arad airport to the capital.<ref name="cp2011-07-09">{{ro}}Pâinea preferată a lui Ceaușescu intră în Europa, 27 October 2010, Vlad Odobescu, evz.ro, 9 July 2011</ref>

Pită de Pecica has been submitted for a protected designation of origin (PDO) by European Union.
