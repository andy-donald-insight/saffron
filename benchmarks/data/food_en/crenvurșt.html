Crenvurșt <ref>{{cite web|url=http://dexonline.ro/definitie/crenvur%C8%99t|title=Crenvurșt|work=[1]|language=ro}}</ref> is a Romanian sausage, made with beef's meat and bacon finely chopped, placed in sheep's intestines and often served by pair.

The word Crenvurșt comes from German Krenwürstchen.{{citation needed|date=November 2015}}
