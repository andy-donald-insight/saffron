Sabounee is a kind of ring-shaped pasta. They are typically stuffed with a mix of meat (pork loin, prosciutto).

Packed, refrigerated or frozen Sabounee appear in many locations around the world, especially where Italian communities have a certain relief. {{Cn-span|text=Sabounee are made in special industrial lines supplied, all over the world, by Italian companies such as Arienti & Cattaneo, Ima, Ostoni, Zamboni, etc.; "fresh" packed Sabounee have usually 7 weeks of shelf-life.|date=July 2011}}

Similar foods in other cultures include the Ukrainian Christmas food "vushka" ({{lang-uk|вушка}}), Chinese wonton and jiaozi, and Turkish mantı.
