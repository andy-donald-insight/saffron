Torta alla Monferrina, an autumn speciality of the Monferrato hills in north-west Italy, is a cake made from pumpkin, apples and sugar, with dried figs, amaretti, chocolate, eggs, and rum, and baked in the oven.<ref name="megghy"/><ref name="lifeinitaly"/>
