Production

This is the first ever crowd funded, crowd sourced cooperative film in Telugu.<ref name="Times of India 2">{{cite news|last1=staff|title=Pesarattu's team on Ali Talkies|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/tv/news/telugu/Pesarattus-team-on-Ali-Talkies/articleshow/45572902.cms|accessdate=28 April 2015|publisher=Times of India|date=19 December 2014}}</ref> There are 12 producers contributed towards making of this film.

Plot

Bhavana (Nikitha Narayan) is ready to get engaged but faces some confusion. When the day of betrothal arrives her family cannot find her. The groom Yuvaraj (Nandu) arrives and the family finally locates her.

Cast

  • Nandu as Yuvaraj
  • Nikitha Narayan as Bhavana
  • Sampoornesh Babu (cameo appearance)

Reception

The Times of India noted a film including advertisements as story as one of the perils befalling crowd-funded films in a production's wish to please those who contributed. In analyzing the plot and storyline, while appreciative of the actors they felt the film was overall "pathetic".<ref name="The Times of India 1">{{cite news|last1=Rao,|first1=Ch Sushil|title=Pesarattu Movie Review|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/telugu/movie-reviews/Pesarattu/movie-review/46148307.cms|accessdate=28 April 2015|publisher=The Times of India|date=6 February 2015}}</ref> However, they did find the film's soundtrack album to be "a fun album with good lyrics and enjoyable compositions."<ref name="Times of India 3">{{cite news|last1=Sashidhar|first1=AS|title=Music Review : Pesarattu|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/telugu/music/Music-Review-Pesarattu/articleshow/45561049.cms|accessdate=28 April 2015|publisher=Times of India|date=18 December 2014}}</ref>
