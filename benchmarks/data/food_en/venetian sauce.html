Venetian sauce (French: Sauce vénitienne) is a classical French herb sauce used to accompany fish.<ref>Auguste Escoffier (1907), Le Guide culinaire</ref> It consists of:

  • a velouté and fish fumet base
  • equal quantities of tarragon vinegar and white wine reduced with:
  • chopped shallots and chervil
  • White wine sauce
  • Herb juice

strained and finished with:

  • chopped chervil and tarragon
