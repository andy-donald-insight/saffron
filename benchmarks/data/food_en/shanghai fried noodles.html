Shanghai fried noodles

Shanghai fried noodles ({{zh|c=上海粗炒|p=Shànghǎi Cūchǎo}}) is a dish made from Shanghai-style noodles, which can be found in most Chinese food markets. The more commonly known Japanese udon can be used as a substitute. The noodles are stir-fried with beef cutlets, bok choy, and onion. The dish is a staple of Shanghai cuisine, which is usually served at dumpling houses.
