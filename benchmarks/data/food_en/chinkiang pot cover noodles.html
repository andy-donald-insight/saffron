Legends

In ancient time, there is a family in Chinkiang. When the wife cooked noddles for her husband, the pot cover of the cooking stove slipped into the noodles pot.However,the noodles were more delicious, neither hard nor lousy.Therefore, he opened a noodle store with a partner.People loved to call it "the pot cover noodles".
