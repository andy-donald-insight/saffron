Malvern pudding is a traditional pudding from the English town of Malvern in Worcestershire.<ref>Information Britain Retrieved 5 June 2010</ref> It is a baked dish made with apples or other seasonal fruit and custard although variations exist.<ref>BBC Hereford and Worcester Retrieved 5 June 2010</ref><ref>Lifestylefood.com Retrieved 5 June 2010</ref><ref>TheFold.com Retrieved 5 June 2010</ref><ref>Greenchronicle.com Retrieved 5 June 2010 </ref><ref>Great British Puddings.com Retrieved 12 May 2012</ref>

It was listed as one of the ten most "threatened puddings" after a survey conducted by UKTV Food.<ref>Wainwright, Martin, 24 July 2006. "Chips are down for Britain's classic dishes" (The Guardian). Retrieved 5 July 2010.</ref>
