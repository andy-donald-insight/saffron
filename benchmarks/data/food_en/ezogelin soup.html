Ezogelin soup or Ezo gelin soup ({{lang-tr|Ezogelin çorbası}}, "the soup of Ezo the bride") is a common soup in Turkish cuisine. The main ingredients are bulgur and red lentils. The origin of the soup is attributed to Ezo the bride from Gaziantep.<ref>Origin of Ezogelin soup, and vegan, gluten-free version of the recipe.</ref>

The ingredients are red lentils, rice, bulgur, olive oil, butter, onion, garlic, tomato, tomato paste, paprika, hot pepper, dried mint, black pepper, and salt; it is usually served with lemon wedges.
