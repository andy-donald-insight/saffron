History

Honey Dew Donuts, Andover St. Danvers, Massachusetts

Honey Dew Donuts was founded on Church Street in Mansfield, Massachusetts, by Richard J. Bowen in 1973. In 1975, a customer suggested bringing a Honey Dew Donuts location to his community, who later became the first franchisee.

Bowen went by the nickname "Dick." He was introduced to the aspect of donuts at the age of twelve by working with his father making donuts in a local donut shop in his community. He discovered his desire for a career in entrepreneurship while attending Stoughton High School, from which he graduated in 1965. After graduating from high school, Bowen enlisted in the army. He was relocated to Germany because of his deployment, where he worked as a donut baker and mechanic.<ref name=AboutHistory>{{cite web |title=The History of Honey Dew |url=http://honeydewdonuts.com/about-us/the-history-of-honey-dew |publisher=Honey Dew Donuts |accessdate=April 17, 2013}}</ref>

Today, Honey Dew Donuts has more than 150 franchises in New England.<ref name="Shops">{{cite web |url=http://www.honeydewdonuts.com/shop-locator |title=Shop locator |publisher=Honey Dew Donuts |accessdate=July 6, 2012}}</ref> The Quincy, Massachusetts YMCA location does not sell donuts due to their focus on health;<ref>Doughnut-less Honey Dew to open in new Quincy YMCA</ref> however, their low fat muffins contain more calories than the average donut.<ref>New donut shop opening in gym won't sell donuts</ref>

Information

Honey Dew Donuts is known to sell donuts, pastries, muffins, and a variety of coffees.<ref name=CranstonPatch>{{cite web |title=BAKERIES, FOOD & DINING, COFFEE AND TEA SHOPS Honey Dew Donuts |url=http://cranston.patch.com/listings/honey-dew-donuts-22 |publisher=Cranston Patch |accessdate=April 17, 2013}}</ref> Their motto is "Always Fresh, Always Good!". The original Honey Dew Donuts location is in Mansfield, Massachusetts. The original Plainville, Massachusetts location was one of the first establishments in New England that constructed and utilized a drive-thru.{{Citation needed|date=May 2015}} In 2003, the franchise had 140 shops, 65 franchise families, and over 20 corporate employees. In 2012, Olivia Culpo, a Miss Rhode Island and Miss Universe winner, was featured in a Honey Dew Donuts commercial.<ref name=ABOUTUS>{{cite web |title=History of Honey Dew Donuts |url=http://honeydewdonuts.com/about-us/the-history-of-honey-dew |publisher=Honey Dew Donuts |accessdate=April 17, 2013}}</ref>
