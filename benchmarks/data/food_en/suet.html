Sylhet Engineering College (SEC) ( সিলেট প্রকৌশল কলেজ ), is an Undergraduate Engineering College in Sylhet, Bangladesh. It is a Public College which was established in 2007. It is affiliated with Shahjalal University of Science and Technology

Campus

The Sylhet Engineering College (SEC) campus is located at Tilagarh, approximately six kilometers away from the heart of Sylhet city. SEC has three large academic buildings, library and computer building, administrive building, the principal's residence, teachers and staff quarters. There are two male hostels and a female hostel for Students.

Departments

  1. Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering.
  2. Department of Computer Science and Engineering.
  3. Department of Civil Engineering.

Each department offers 4 years B.Sc (Engineering) degree every year.

Computer Science and Engineering Department -Sylhet Engineering College

Postgraduate studies and more engineering departments are going to be introduced soon. It is expected that, Sylhet Engineering College will be upgraded to a full-fledged public university soon and will be named Sylhet University of Engineering and Technology(SUET).

Admission Process

Students who have passed the SSC and HSC from Science department may be admitted by admission test with total GPA. Academic activities are completed with four year semester credit system.

Campus Life

Classes are held everyday from 8am to 5pm without weekly holiday (Friday). All academic activities are maintained by administrive office. Educational tour was organized for the students every year. Various cultural activities and special days are celebrated in this campus. e.g. International Mother language day, Independence day, Victory day and pohela baishakh.

Campus Radio

Bangladesh's 1st campus based online radio known as Campus Radio was originally broadcast by Sylhet Engineering College. Now it is maintained by Department of Computer Science and Engineering (SEC).

Central Library

Central library is situated besides Academic office.
