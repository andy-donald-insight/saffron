{{Infobox prepared food | name = Campanelle | image = | caption = Campanelle with summer vegetables and grated cheese | alternate_name = | country = Italy | region = Italy | creator = | course = | type = Pasta | served = | main_ingredient = wheat | variations = | calories = | other = }} Campanelle {{IPA-it|kampaˈnɛlle|}} (Italian for "bellflowers"<ref name= "barilla"/> or "little bells"), is a type of pasta which is shaped like a cone with a ruffled edge,<ref name= "pastafits"/> or a bell-like flower. It is also sometimes referred to as gigli or riccioli.{{cn|date=May 2014}} It is intended to be served with a thick sauce,<ref name= "barilla"/> or in a casserole.

Campanelle can also refer to "handbells." <ref name= "geek"/>
