Malapua is a village in Ancuabe District in Cabo Delgado Province in northeastern Mozambique.<ref>{{cite web| url=http://geonames.nga.mil/ggmagaz/geonames4.asp |title=NGA GeoName Database |publisher=National Geospatial Intelligence Agency |accessdate=2008-06-26 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080608190852/http://geonames.nga.mil/ggmagaz/geonames4.asp <!-- Bot retrieved archive --> |archivedate = 2008-06-08}}</ref>

It is located several miles north-west of the district capital of Ancuabe.
