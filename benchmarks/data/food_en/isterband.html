Isterband with fried onion rings, pickled cucumber and creamed potatoes.

Isterband (Swedish: "lard-strips") is a coarsely ground, lightly smoked sausage from Sweden.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.spisa.nu/action/subProductGroup?moreProductGroups=true&productGroupId3=69&productGroupId4=14376|title=Isterband|work=Spisa.nu|language=Swedish|archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6JBpv19qr|archivedate=27 August 2013}}</ref> It is made of pork, barley groats and potato. There are many varieties of isterband, such as "småländska isterband" from the region of Småland, "syrliga isterband" with a slightly sour taste, and "lättisterband" with a low calorie content. Isterband is often served together with creamed dill potatoes and pickled beetroot.
