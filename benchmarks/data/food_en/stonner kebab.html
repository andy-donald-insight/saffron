The stonner kebab is a saveloy wrapped in strips of doner meat, coated in two layers of batter, and then deep fried.{{sfn|Moore|2011|p=176}} It is served on a bed of chips. The kebab weighs 1.4 kilograms.{{sfn|The Sun|2004}} The dish is available in Ruby Chip Shop in Glasgow.{{sfn|Crabb|2004}}{{sfn|The Sun|2004}}{{sfn|Sky News|2004}} The name "stonner" is derived from a combination of "sausage" and "donner", and is the Glaswegian slang word for an erection.{{sfn|Crabb|2004}}{{sfn|Moore|2011|p=176}}

The stonner kebab shot to fame because of its high calorie count of 1,000 calories, and 46 grams of fat.{{sfn|Crabb|2004}}{{sfn|The Sun|2004}}
