Taglierini {{IPA-it|taʎʎeˈriːni|}} is a type of ribbon pasta. It is like tagliatelle but is a thinner version. It is a traditional recipe in Piedmont where it is called Tajarin<ref>http://lacucinaitalianamagazine.com/glossary/T</ref&gt; and made of egg dough (pasta all'uovo).<ref>http://italianfood.about.com/od/meatsauces/r/blr0746.htm</ref&gt; It is typically served with butter and truffles (tajarin ai tartufi)<ref>http://www.epicurean.com/featured/tajarin-with-fresh-white-truffle.html</ref&gt; or meat roast sauce (tajarin al sugo di arrosto).<ref>http://italianfood.about.com/od/meatsauces/r/blr0747.htm</ref&gt;
