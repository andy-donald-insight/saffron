Tapalapa bread is a traditional bread, mainly in Senegal, The Gambia and Guinea.

In 2009 in The Gambia, a traditional method of production by street vendors and distributors was criticised as being unhygienic.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://allafrica.com/stories/200906030693.html|title=Gambia: Concern Raised Over Tapalapa Preparation|last=Fadera|first=Hatab|newspaper=The Daily Observer|publisher=Observer Company Ltd|date=3 June 2009|accessdate=25 June 2012}}</ref>
