Rice bread is a type of bread that is made from rice flour rather than wheat flour.<ref>{{cite news |title=Rice bread baking machine a hit for Japan firm |first=Elaine |last=Lies |first2=Miral |last2=Fahmy |url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2010/11/26/us-japan-breadmaker-idUSTRE6AP33T20101126?irpc=932 |agency=Reuters |date=Nov 26, 2010 |accessdate=May 14, 2011}}</ref> Being gluten free,<ref>{{cite web |url=http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/easy-rice-bread/Detail.aspx |title=Easy Rice Bread |first=Lindsay |last=Schott |work=Allrecipes.com |accessdate=May 14, 2011}}</ref> it will not cause adverse reactions for people with gluten intolerance.

The Vietnamese banh mi (baguette) is traditionally made with a mixture of wheat and rice flour or sometimes the latter, resulting in an airy, crispy texture.<ref>http://www.postcity.com/Eat-Shop-Do/Eat/May-2012/Wicked-wich-of-the-East-Six-of-the-best-places-to-try-a-banh-mi-sandwich/</ref&gt;

In 2001, a study group at Yamagata University's Department of Engineering used foam molding technology to produce bread made from 100% rice flour.<ref>ぷらっとフォーム 米粉100%のパン</ref><ref>山形大学大学院理工学研究科准教授 西岡昭博</ref>
