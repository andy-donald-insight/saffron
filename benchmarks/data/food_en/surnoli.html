Surnoli is a kind of Pancake popular among the Konkani community. They have a puffy texture with holes, with a yellow color and are usually made about 10&nbsp;inches in diameter. Surnolis are traditionally eaten for breakfast or afternoon tea, served with homemade butter.

Preparation

Surnoli batter is made by grinding together soaked rice mixed with poha, coconut, buttermilk, jaggery and turmeric into a smooth paste. The batter is cooked covered on a girdle on one side.
