Secca de bœuf or Secca d'Entrevaux is a type of dried salted beef made in Entrevaux. Similar to the Swiss viande des Grisons or Bündnerfleisch, it is typically eaten as a starter, thinly sliced and served with virgin olive oil, lemon juice, and sliced tomatoes.
