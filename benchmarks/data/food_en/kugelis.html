Kugelis (also bulvių plokštainis, literally "flat potato dish" or banda - a dialecticism frequent especially in Dzūkija region) is a baked potato pudding. The main ingredients are potatoes, bacon, milk, onions, and eggs. It may be spiced with salt, black pepper, bay leaves, and/or marjoram. It is usually eaten with apple sauce, lingonberry preserve, sour cream, or crumbled fried fat—from white bacon (spirgai) or pork.<ref name="Bremzen90">{{cite book|last=von Bremzen, Anya; Welchman, John|title=Please to the Table: The Russian Cookbook|publisher=Workman Publishing|location=New York|year=1990|pages=319–20|isbn=0-89480-845-1}}</ref>

Similar dishes are the Jewish kugel and Belarusian potato babka.<ref name="Bremzen90"/>
