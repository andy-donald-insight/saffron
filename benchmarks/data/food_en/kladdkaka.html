Kladdkaka (lit. Gooey cake but more commonly known as "chocolate mud cake") is a type of Swedish cake. This dense sticky chocolate cake is similar to a brownie and has a soft and gooey center. It is sometimes eaten with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.

The cake may have inspired the brownie or a similar French recipe.<ref>{{cite web|title=KLADDKAKA – 7 SAKER DU INTE VISSTE OM DEN|url=http://mittkok.expressen.se/artikel/kladdkaka-7-saker-du-inte-visste-om-den/?sida=2#bildspel|website=Mitt Kök (Swedish)|accessdate=9 May 2015}}</ref>
