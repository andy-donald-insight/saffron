{{Infobox Korean name |img=Korean_soup-Gulgukbap-01.jpg |caption= |hangul=굴국밥 |hanja=none |rr=gulgukbap |mr=kulkukpap |}} Gulgukbap (literally "oyster soup with rice") is a Korean soup made with oysters, mu (daikon), green chili pepper, onion root,<!--What is onion root? There are only tiny hairs as roots coming out from the base of an onion--> anchovy and sliced shiitake mushrooms. The dish is commonly considered as a variety of haejangguk (soup believed to mitigate hangovers) or boyangsik (hangul: 보양식, supplement food for health).
