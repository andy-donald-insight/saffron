About

An almojábana is a small, bun-shaped bread with a tart flavor. In Cali and Valle de Cauca (around Cali) it is called pan de bono and are bagel-shaped instead of bun-shaped.<ref name=columbia />

Ingredients

Almojábanas are made with masarepa or pre-cooked white cornmeal, cottage cheese, butter, baking powder, salt, eggs and milk.
