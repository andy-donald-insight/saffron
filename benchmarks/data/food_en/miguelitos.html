Miguelitos are a type of cake made in La Roda, in Castile-La Mancha, Spain.

Description

They are a quite simple traditional cake consisting of soft puff pastry with a creamy custard-like filling and covered with sugar powder.

In La Mancha Miguelitos are usually eaten along with café con leche.
