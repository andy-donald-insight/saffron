Leek soup is a soup based on potatoes, leeks, broth<ref>{{cite web|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=cDNRHs_f5P8C&pg=PA71|title=Not Your Mother's Slow Cooker Cookbook|publisher=|page=71|accessdate=2 December 2014}}</ref> (usually chicken), and heavy cream.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=-VxldfaFXDAC&pg=PA56|title=Bouchon|publisher=|page=56|accessdate=2 December 2014}}</ref> Other ingredients used may be salt and pepper, and various spices.

Generally the potatoes are diced and cooked in broth, while the leeks are chopped and sautéed. After this all ingredients are combined and stirred. Chives may be used for garnish.

Leek soup is strongly associated with Wales, and is an important item of Welsh cuisine. In Romania, this soup is popular and known as Ciorbă de praz.
