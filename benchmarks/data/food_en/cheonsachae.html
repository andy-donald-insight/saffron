Cheonsachae are Korean half-transparent noodles made from the jelly-like extract left after steaming kombu, without the addition of grain flour or starch. The taste of these noodles is bland, so they are generally eaten as a light salad after seasoned or served as a garnish beneath saengseon hoe (sliced raw fish). Cheonsachae has a chewy texture and is low in calories.<ref name="Cheonsachae">{{cite news|url=http://www.encyber.com/search_w/ctdetail.php?masterno=798768&contentno=798768 |script-title=ko:천사채 |publisher=Doosan Encyclopedia |language=Korean}}</ref>
