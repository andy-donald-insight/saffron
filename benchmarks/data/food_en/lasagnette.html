Lasagnette is a type of ribbon pasta noodles. It is a narrower version of lasagna. Characteristics of Lasagnette differ based on the form of their edges. Different kinds could have edges with a waved cut on both sides, straight cut edges on both side, or a variation including one side with a straight cut and the other with a waved cut.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Lasagnette Noodles|url = http://www.recipetips.com/glossary-term/t--36410/lasagnette-noodles.asp|accessdate = 2015-09-30}}</ref> Lasagnette can be prepared in various forms; the two most popular involve a thinner version of the traditional layered Italian lasagna. The second version combines ingredients of the recipe with the noodle, and is served tossed on a plate. <ref>{{Cite web|title = What Is a Lasagnette? (with pictures)|url = http://www.wisegeek.com/what-is-a-lasagnette.htm|accessdate = 2015-09-30}}</ref>
