Kupati

Kupati ({{lang-ka|კუპატი}}) is a type of Georgian<ref>The Georgian Feast: The Vibrant Culture and Savory Food of the Republic of Georgia, Darra Goldstein, p. 95</ref><ref>Georgia, Armenia & Azerbaijan, John Noble, Michael Kohn, Danielle Systermans, p. 46</ref><ref>Кавказская кухня. Мясные блюда, p. 7792</ref><ref>Новые слова и значения, Надежда Захаровна Котелова, Евгений Александрович Левашов, p. 312</ref> sausage that is made from pork. It is popular in the Caucasus region.
