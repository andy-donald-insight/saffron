Scorcolga or Scordolea is a thick sauce of Romanian cuisine from the region of Dobrogea consisting of a puree of walnuts and bread mashed with milk, salt, pepper, lemon juice and oil. The scorcolga is traditionally served with crayfish.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.saveursdumonde.net/pays/saveurs-de-roumanie/|title=Saveurs de Roumanie |publisher=|language=fr}}</ref>
