Prežganka ("browned" soup with eggs) is Slovenian national soup made of flour, caraway seeds and beaten eggs.<ref>Traditional slovenian cookery. Adamlje Slavko, 1997. Mladinska knjiga. ISBN 8611150449</ref> The brown color comes from browning the flour in oil or butter. Instead of the flour, breadcrumbs can be used.

Prežganka is a traditional dish from Upper Carniola region.
