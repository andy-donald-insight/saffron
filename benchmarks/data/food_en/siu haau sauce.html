{{norefs|date=May 2010}} {{Chinese|s=烧烤酱|t=燒烤醬|j=siu1 haau1 zoeng3|p=shāokǎo jiàng|l=barbecue sauce}} Siu haau sauce is the primary barbecue sauce used in Chinese and Cantonese cuisine. It is used during the barbecue-cooking process as opposed to a flavoring sauce after the food is made.

Siu haau sauce is not used for siu mei rotisserie style cooking; dishes such as char siu each have their own sauce.
